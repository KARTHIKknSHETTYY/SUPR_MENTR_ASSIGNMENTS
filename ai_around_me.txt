Description: Real-life AI applications used daily and how Machine Learning works behind them.

1. Google Maps
I use Google Maps to find directions and avoid traffic. ML analyzes traffic data from millions of users to predict congestion and suggest the fastest route.

2. YouTube Recommendations
YouTube suggests videos based on what I watch. ML models learn from my watch history, likes, and viewing time to recommend videos I might enjoy.

3. Google Search
When I search for something, Google shows the most relevant results. ML helps understand the meaning of my query and ranks web pages that best match it.

4. Email Spam Filter (Gmail)
Gmail automatically moves spam emails to the spam folder. ML models are trained to detect patterns commonly found in spam messages.

5. Voice Assistants (Google Assistant / Siri)
Voice assistants understand spoken commands. ML converts speech into text and interprets the user's request to perform actions like setting alarms or searching information.

6. Netflix Recommendations
Netflix recommends movies and shows based on my viewing habits. ML analyzes what I watch, rate, or skip to suggest similar content.

7. Face Unlock on Smartphones
Many phones unlock using face recognition. ML models analyze facial features and compare them with stored data to verify the user.

8. Instagram / Facebook Feed
The posts I see are arranged based on my interests. ML predicts which posts I am most likely to interact with and shows them first.

9. Online Shopping Recommendations (Amazon / Flipkart)
Shopping apps recommend products based on my browsing and purchase history. ML identifies patterns in user behavior to suggest relevant items.

10. Autocorrect and Predictive Text (Keyboard)
My phone suggests the next word while typing. ML models learn from language patterns and previous typing behavior to predict the next word.
